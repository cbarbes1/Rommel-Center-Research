AB We propose a notion of Frobenius-Perron dimension for certain free Z-modules of infinite rank and compute it for the Z-modules of finite dimensional complex representations of unitary groups with nonnegative dominant weights. The definition of Frobenius-Perron dimension that we are introducing naturally generalizes the well-known Frobenius-Perron dimension on the category of finite dimensional complex representations of a finite group.
