AB Let Gr(k, n) be the Grassmannian. The quantum multiplication by the first Chern class c(1)(Gr(k, n)) induces an endomorphism (c) over cap (1) of the finite-dimensional vector space QH*(Gr(k, n))(vertical bar q=1) specialized at q = 1. Our main result is a case that a conjecture by Galkin holds. It states that the largest real eigenvalue of (c) over cap (1) is greater than or equal to dim Gr(k, n)+1 with equality if and only if Gr(k, n) = Pn-1.
