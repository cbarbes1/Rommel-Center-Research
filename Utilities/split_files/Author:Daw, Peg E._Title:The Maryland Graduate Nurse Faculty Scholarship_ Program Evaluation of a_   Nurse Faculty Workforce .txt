AB Strategies to address the nurse faculty shortage have been implemented over the past decade with mixed results. Four-year outcomes of a statewide nursing scholarship-for-service program to support future educators are reported. Over $11 million was awarded to 250 recipients who are repaying their obligation through teaching. Recommendations for programmatic changes to improve cost-effectiveness are provided.
