TI When is the <i>q</i>-multiplicity of a weight a power of <i>q</i>?
SO ELECTRONIC JOURNAL OF COMBINATORICS
LA English
DT Article
ID ADJOINT REPRESENTATION; FIBONACCI; FORMULA
AB Berenshtein and Zelevinskii provided an exhaustive list of pairs of weights (lambda,mu) of simple Lie algebras g (up to Dynkin diagram isomorphism) for which the multiplicity of the weight mu in the representation of g with highest weight lambda is equal to one. Using Kostant's weight multiplicity formula we describe and enumerate the contributing terms to the multiplicity for subsets of these pairs of weights and show that, in these cases, the cardinality of these contributing sets is enumerated by (multiples of) Fibonacci numbers. We conclude by using these results to compute the associated q-multiplicity for the pairs of weights considered, and conjecture that in all cases the q-multiplicity of such pairs of weights is given by a power of q.
