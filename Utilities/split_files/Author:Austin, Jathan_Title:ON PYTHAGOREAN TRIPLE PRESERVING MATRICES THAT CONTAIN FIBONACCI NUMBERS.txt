AB In this paper, we show how to use Pythagorean triples whose entries can be expressed using Fibonacci numbers to construct Pythagorean triple preserving matrices with entries that can also be expressed using Fibonacci numbers. We conclude with another Pythagorean triple preserving matrix whose powers contain Fibonacci numbers.
