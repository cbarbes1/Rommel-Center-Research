DE quantum cohomology; Frobenius-Perron dimension; odd quadrics
AB Let H center dot(OG) be the quantum cohomology (specialized at q = 1) of the (2n-1)- dimensional quadric OG. We will calculate the characteristic polynomial of the linear operators induced by quantum multiplication in H center dot(OG) and the Frobenius- Perron dimension. We also check that Galkin's lower bound conjecture holds for OG.
