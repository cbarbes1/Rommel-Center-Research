ID OUTCOMES
AB Statewide initiatives to address registered nurse and nursing faculty shortages funded through a percentage of pooled hospital-based revenue are described and evaluated. From 2016-2020, $90 million was awarded to 24 nursing programs for 106 competitive institutional grants and 532 faculty to prepare for or advance in teaching roles. Outcomes included increasing doctoral degree conferrals by 73% and adding 162 full-time and 190 part-time faculty to the workforce.
