AB Social media is a part of life these days. A majority of students use social media as a way to interact with each other, get information and keep up with current events. As teachers, we can embrace this and help guide our students on how to properly use social media as digital citizens. This is how two professionals use Twitter to engage themselves and their students in professional learning networks (PLNs).
